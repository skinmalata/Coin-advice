# How to Use DeBank for DeFi: Complete Guide 2026

DeBank has become the go-to dashboard for tracking DeFi positions across multiple blockchains. Instead of checking 10 different protocols manually, DeBank aggregates all your positions in one beautiful interface.

This comprehensive guide explains how to set up DeBank, track your DeFi portfolio, and use advanced features to manage your decentralized finance positions.

## What is DeBank?

DeBank is a Web3 dashboard that connects to your wallet and shows all your DeFi positions across 40+ blockchains and 1000+ protocols.

### What DeBank Tracks:
- **Lending**: Aave, Compound, MakerDAO positions
- **Liquidity Providing**: Uniswap, SushiSwap, Curve pools
- **Staking**: Lido, Rocket Pool, native staking
- **Yield Farming**: Yearn, Beefy, Autofarm vaults
- **Bridged Assets**: Tokens moved across chains
- **NFTs**: Collections across multiple networks

## Setting Up DeBank

### Step 1: Access DeBank

1. Visit [debank.com](https://debank.com/)
2. Click "Launch App" or "Connect Wallet"
3. **Never click links in DMs**—always type debank.com directly

### Step 2: Connect Your Wallet

**Supported Wallets:**
- **MetaMask** (browser extension or mobile)
- **WalletConnect** (Rainbow, Trust, etc.)
- **Coinbase Wallet**
- **Ledger** (via MetaMask or WalletConnect)
- **Phantom** (Solana support)

Select your wallet and approve the connection.

### Step 3: View Your Dashboard

Once connected, DeBank automatically shows:
1. **Total Portfolio Value**: Sum of all tracked assets
2. **Chain Breakdown**: Balance per blockchain
3. **Protocol Breakdown**: Positions per DeFi protocol
4. **Token List**: All tokens with amounts and values
5. **NFT Gallery**: Your NFTs across chains

## Managing Lending Positions (Aave Example)

### Viewing Your Aave Position:

1. Click "Aave" in the protocol list
2. See supplied tokens (USDC, ETH, etc.) with:
   - **Supply APY**: Interest earning
   - **Collateral Factor**: How much you can borrow
   - **Rewards**: AAVE token rewards
3. Check "Health Factor" (must stay above 1.0 to avoid liquidation)

### Adjusting Position:

1. Click "Manage" on any supplied token
2. Choose "Withdraw" or "Supply More"
3. Approve transaction in your wallet
4. Confirm and wait for blockchain confirmation

**Liquidation Warning**: If Health Factor drops below 1.0, your position gets liquidated. Monitor closely!

## Managing Liquidity Positions (Uniswap Example)

### Viewing Your LP Position:

1. Click "Uniswap" in protocols
2. See LP tokens (e.g., ETH/USDC pair) with:
   - **Pool Share**: Your percentage of the pool
   - **Unclaimed Fees**: Trading fees earned
   - **Impermanent Loss**: Current vs holding separately
3. Check "Remove Liquidity" to exit

### Removing Liquidity:

1. Click "Manage" on the LP position
2. Select percentage to remove (25%, 50%, 100%)
3. Approve and confirm transaction
4. Receive both tokens based on pool ratio

**Impermanent Loss Warning**: Can result in less value than holding. Model outcomes with our [Coin Advice Profit Calculator](../../index.html).

## Security Tips for Using DeBank

### 1. Connect Only Your Own Wallets
- Never connect to DMs claiming "verify your wallet"
- Only connect at debank.com (type it directly)
- Use [Ledger](https://www.ledger.com/) for signing transactions

### 2. Be Careful with Approvals
- DeBank is read-only (doesn't need transaction signing)
- When you click "Manage" and go to protocol, that site needs approval
- Revoke unused approvals via [revoke.cash](https://revoke.cash/)

### 3. Monitor Health Factors
- Keep lending health factor above 2.0 (safety buffer)
- Set alerts for when health factor drops below 1.5
- Add collateral if approaching liquidation

### 4. Verify Token Contracts
Before interacting with any token shown in DeBank:
- Check contract on [Etherscan](https://etherscan.io/)
- Use our [Coin Advice Token Checker](../../index.html) for security analysis
- Beware of fake tokens with similar names
