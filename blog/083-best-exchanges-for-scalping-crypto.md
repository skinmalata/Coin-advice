# Best Exchanges for Scalping Crypto in 2026

Scalping is a trading strategy that aims to profit from small price movements, often executing dozens or hundreds of trades within minutes. Success requires lightning-fast execution, minimal fees, and deep liquidity.

This guide identifies the best exchanges for scalping cryptocurrency in 2026, helping you choose a platform that won't slow you down.

## What Makes a Good Scalping Exchange?

Scalpers need specific features that differ from swing or position traders:

- **Low Latency**: Orders execute in milliseconds, not seconds
- **Minimal Fees**: High-frequency trading amplifies even small fee differences
- **Deep Liquidity**: Large order books prevent slippage on quick trades
- **Stable API**: For algorithmic scalping bots
- **Real-Time Charts**: [TradingView](https://www.tradingview.com/) integration essential
- **High Uptime**: Even 1 minute of downtime can cost scalpers dearly

## Scalping Risks and Risk Management

### The Dangers of Scalping
1. **Overtrading**: Taking too many trades and burning fees
2. **Emotional Trading**: Revenge trading after losses
3. **Leverage Risk**: High leverage = rapid liquidations
4. **Slippage**: Fast markets can fill you at worse prices
5. **Tax Implications**: Hundreds of trades = complex tax reporting

### Risk Management for Scalpers
- **Risk 0.5-1% per trade** (not 2-5% like swing traders)
- **Use stop-losses** on every single trade
- **Take profits quickly** (scalping is about volume, not home runs)
- **Limit daily trades** to avoid overtrading
- **Withdraw profits** regularly to a [Ledger hardware wallet](https://www.ledger.com/)

5. **Set Daily Limits**: Stop after X% profit or Y number of trades
