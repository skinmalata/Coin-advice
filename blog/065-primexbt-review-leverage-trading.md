## Leverage Trading Explained

Before diving into PrimeXBT's features, let's clarify leverage trading for those unfamiliar with the concept.

Leverage allows you to open positions larger than your account balance by borrowing funds from the exchange. For example:
- With 100x leverage, a $100 deposit controls a $10,000 position
- A 1% price move in your favor yields 100% profit on your margin
- However, a 1% move against you results in total loss of your margin

**Warning**: Leverage trading is extremely risky and leads to rapid losses for inexperienced traders. Never use leverage you don't fully understand.
