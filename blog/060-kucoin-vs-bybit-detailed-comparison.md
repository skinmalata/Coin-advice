# KuCoin vs Bybit: Detailed Comparison 2026

KuCoin and Bybit are two of the most popular cryptocurrency exchanges, each with distinct strengths. KuCoin is known as "the people's exchange" for its massive altcoin selection and trading bots, while Bybit has built a reputation for excellent derivatives trading and copy trading features.

This detailed comparison breaks down every aspect of both platforms to help you decide which one aligns with your trading goals. Whether you're an altcoin enthusiast, a derivatives trader, or a beginner looking for user-friendly features, we've got you covered.

## Company Background

### KuCoin
Launched in 2017, KuCoin is headquartered in Seychelles and serves over 30 million users worldwide. The platform is known for listing new altcoins early, often before they hit larger exchanges. KuCoin offers spot trading, futures, staking, and automated trading bots.

### Bybit
Founded in 2018 and headquartered in Dubai, Bybit has quickly grown to serve over 20 million users. Originally focused on derivatives trading, Bybit has expanded to offer spot trading, copy trading, and earn products. The platform is known for its robust infrastructure and 24/7 customer support.

## Supported Cryptocurrencies

### KuCoin
KuCoin supports over 700 cryptocurrencies, including many low-cap altcoins and new project launches. The platform's GemHunter feature helps users discover emerging projects early. If you're looking to trade beyond Bitcoin and Ethereum, KuCoin offers one of the widest selections in the industry.

### Bybit
Bybit supports over 600 cryptocurrencies, focusing more on established coins and popular altcoins. While the selection is smaller than KuCoin's, Bybit's listing process is more rigorous, reducing the risk of listing scams or low-quality projects.

**Winner**: KuCoin for altcoin variety, Bybit for quality control.

**Winner**: KuCoin for automated trading bots, Bybit for copy trading and derivatives.

## User Experience

### KuCoin Interface
KuCoin's interface is functional but can feel cluttered, especially for beginners. The platform offers a "Lite" mode that simplifies trading, but advanced features are still accessible. The mobile app is well-designed and offers full feature parity with the desktop version.

### Bybit Interface
Bybit's interface is cleaner and more intuitive than KuCoin's, making it easier for beginners to navigate. The platform offers classic and advanced trading views, with customizable layouts. Bybit's mobile app is among the best in the industry, with smooth performance and all features available.

**Winner**: Bybit for user experience and beginner-friendliness.

## Security

### KuCoin
- 95% of funds in cold storage
- Two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Anti-phishing codes
- Withdrawal address whitelisting
- Insurance fund for derivatives trading

KuCoin suffered a major hack in 2020, but fully reimbursed all affected users using their insurance fund. Security has been significantly improved since then.

### Bybit
- 100% of funds in cold storage (except operational hot wallet)
- Multi-signature cold wallet technology
- 2FA and hardware security key support
- Regular proof of reserves audits
- $510 million insurance fund for derivatives

**Winner**: Bybit for stronger cold storage practices and regular audits.

For maximum security, store large holdings in a [Ledger hardware wallet](https://www.ledger.com/) rather than leaving them on any exchange.
