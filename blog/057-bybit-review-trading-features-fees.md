# Bybit Review 2026: Trading Features, Fees, and Complete Guide

Bybit has established itself as one of the leading cryptocurrency derivatives and spot trading platforms since its launch in 2018. With a focus on professional trading tools, competitive fees, and innovative features, Bybit has attracted millions of users worldwide. This comprehensive review examines whether Bybit deserves a place in your trading toolkit in 2026.

## Company Background and Trust

Bybit was founded in 2018 by Ben Zhou and has quickly risen to become one of the top derivatives exchanges globally. Headquartered in Dubai with operations across multiple jurisdictions, Bybit has positioned itself as a compliant and transparent platform. The exchange regularly publishes proof of reserves, allowing users to verify that their funds are fully backed.

Unlike some competitors that have faced regulatory challenges, Bybit has proactively engaged with regulators worldwide. The platform holds licenses in several jurisdictions and continues to expand its regulatory compliance framework. This commitment to legitimacy provides users with greater peace of mind when entrusting their funds to the platform.

## Trading Features and Products

Bybit offers a comprehensive suite of trading products catering to different types of traders. Understanding these offerings helps you determine if the platform aligns with your trading style and goals.

### Spot Trading

Bybit's spot trading platform supports over 600 cryptocurrencies, providing access to major coins, emerging altcoins, and new project launches. The interface is intuitive, with real-time order books, trading history, and charting powered by [TradingView](https://www.tradingview.com/). Spot trades settle immediately, giving you actual ownership of the cryptocurrencies you purchase.

The spot trading fees follow a maker-taker model, with makers paying 0.1% and takers paying 0.15%. However, holding Bybit's native token (BIT) or achieving higher trading volumes can significantly reduce these fees. VIP tiers offer progressively lower fees, with the highest tiers paying as little as 0.01% for makers.

### Derivatives Trading

Where Bybit truly shines is in derivatives trading. The platform offers inverse perpetual contracts, USDT perpetual contracts, and inverse futures contracts. These products allow traders to speculate on price movements with leverage up to 100x on select pairs, though such high leverage is not recommended for beginners.

The derivatives platform includes advanced order types like limit, market, stop-loss, take-profit, and trailing stop orders. The matching engine processes up to 100,000 transactions per second, ensuring smooth trading even during high volatility periods. For traders interested in derivatives, [Bybit's affiliate program](https://www.bybit.com/en-US/affiliate) offers up to 50% commission plus CPA rewards.

### Options Trading

Bybit has expanded into crypto options trading, allowing traders to hedge positions or speculate on price movements with defined risk. Options contracts are available on major cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin and Ethereum, with various strike prices and expiration dates.

### Copy Trading

One of Bybit's standout features is its copy trading platform. Novice traders can automatically copy the trades of successful traders, while experienced traders can earn additional income by becoming copy trading masters. The platform transparently displays each trader's historical performance, risk metrics, and trading style.

To start copy trading, browse the leaderboard of master traders. Evaluate their ROI, win rate, maximum drawdown, and trading frequency. Once you find a trader whose strategy aligns with your risk tolerance, allocate funds to copy their trades automatically. This feature is excellent for learning while earning, though past performance doesn't guarantee future results.

## Security Measures

Security is paramount when choosing a cryptocurrency exchange. Bybit employs multiple layers of protection to safeguard user funds and data.

### Cold Storage

The majority of user funds are stored in cold wallets, disconnected from the internet and protected by multi-signature technology. This approach minimizes the risk of hacks, as online hot wallets contain only a small percentage of total funds for daily operations.

### Two-Factor Authentication

Bybit mandates 2FA for all withdrawals and sensitive account changes. The platform supports both authenticator apps and hardware security keys like YubiKey. We strongly recommend using an authenticator app or hardware key rather than SMS authentication, which is vulnerable to SIM swapping attacks.

### Anti-Phishing Measures

Users can set up anti-phishing codes that appear in all official Bybit emails. This feature helps distinguish legitimate communications from phishing attempts. Additionally, withdrawal address whitelisting prevents funds from being sent to unauthorized addresses, even if your account is compromised.

### Insurance Fund

Bybit maintains an insurance fund to cover losses in the event of trader liquidations exceeding the available margin. This fund helps ensure market stability and protects traders from cascading liquidations during extreme volatility.

Beyond active trading, Bybit offers several ways to earn passive income on your cryptocurrency holdings.

Bybit Earn provides flexible and fixed savings products. ### Launchpad and Launchpool

Bybit regularly hosts token launches, allowing users to commit funds to earn allocations of new project tokens. These opportunities can provide significant returns, though they carry the risks associated with new, unproven projects.
