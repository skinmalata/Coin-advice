Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

🔒 Privacy-First Approach

Coin Advice is built with privacy at its core. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. All your data (portfolio, tracked airdrops, calculations) stays in your browser's localStorage.

📊 Data We Don't Collect

  • We don't collect your name, email, or contact info
  • We don't track your IP address or location
  • We don't use cookies for tracking
  • We don't require user registration
  • We don't sell your data to third parties

💼 Local Storage Only

Your portfolio holdings, tracked airdrops, and calculation history are stored only in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

🌐 External APIs

We use third-party APIs (CoinGecko, Binance, DeFiLlama, GoPlus, RapidAPI) to fetch crypto data. These services may have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing:

  • CoinGecko Privacy Policy
  • Binance Privacy Policy
