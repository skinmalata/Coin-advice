About Coin Advice

Empowering crypto traders with free, privacy-first tools

Our Mission

Coin Advice provides free, professional-grade cryptocurrency analysis tools for traders and investors. We believe everyone should have access to real-time market data, portfolio tracking, and security tools without paying expensive subscription fees.

What We Offer

  • Live Price Tracker - Real-time prices from CoinGecko & Binance
  • Trending - Top gainers, losers & trending coins
  • DEX Scanner - Hot pairs from DexScreener across 5+ chains
  • Token Checker - Security audit via GoPlus API
  • Charts - Historical price data from CoinPaprika
  • Arbitrage Scanner - Find price gaps across exchanges
  • Airdrop Finder - Discover potential airdrops via DeFiLlama
  • Profit Calculator - Calculate ROI on your trades
  • Global Stats - Market cap, BTC dominance & metrics

Privacy First

Your data stays in your browser. We don't track you, we don't store your portfolio data on servers, and we don't require registration. Everything is stored locally using localStorage.
